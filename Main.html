How it works

Less food wasted. More food for those that need it!

Our dedicated team of volunteers collects surplus food from our diverse group of donors. These donors include local stores, restaurants, bakeries, hotels, and farmers' markets, all of whom share our goal of reducing food waste and supporting those in need.

Once collected, these donations are carefully sorted and distributed to various charities and organizations within our community.

Making an impact on the community.

Through our collaborative efforts, we ensure that nutritious and wholesome food reaches those who need it most. By facilitating the donation and distribution process, we not only prevent good food from going to waste but also create a sense of community and solidarity.

Together, we make a meaningful impact on the lives of individuals and families in the community.
